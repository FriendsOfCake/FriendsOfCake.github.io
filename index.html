Friends Of Cake

The friendly CakePHP community

Welcome friend !

Friends Of Cake is a community of passionate CakePHP developers, working together to improve the CakePHP community and produce high quality CakePHP plugins.

Community

We want to help build an awesome CakePHP community. A community where people work together, unite their efforts, helps each other out and be all round friendly with each other.

We want to create a community that help promote and unite the CakePHP community, to everyones benefit.

Plugins & Code

We want to create a "go to" place for high quality and well maintained CakePHP plugins.

We aim to unite people around some central, common plugins. Rather than having several forks of a plugin, we want to merge it in to one awesome plugin, everyone can contribute to.

Do you have a plugin that could benefit the community? Want to make it friendly? Get in touch with on in #FriendsOfCake on FreeNode

Projects

Friends Of Cake currently works on theese projects:

  • crud - (formerly jippi/cakephp-crud) - Application development on steroids
  • app-template - An empty 2.4 cakephp application template, for use with composer
  • Built With Cake - A website to showcase awesome CakePHP projects
If you got a project that could fit right in to Friends Of Cake, you can get in touch with us about adopting it into FoC on IRC .
